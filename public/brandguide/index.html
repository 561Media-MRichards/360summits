01 Logo Usage

Logo System

Two logo variants — horizontal and square — serve different contexts. The horizontal logo is the primary mark. The square logo works for social profiles, favicons, and compact layouts.

Do

Use provided SVG files at any scale. Maintain proportions and clear space equal to the height of the diamond mark.

Don't

Do not stretch, rotate, or alter the proportions of the logo. Do not add drop shadows or effects.

Don't

Do not change individual letter colors or swap the purple and blue accent. Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.

Plus Jakarta Sans

The body typeface in action

Plus Jakarta Sans pairs a modern geometric structure with soft, approachable details. Its open apertures ensure excellent readability on both dark and light backgrounds. Use weight 400 for body, 500 for UI labels, and 600-700 for emphasis.
